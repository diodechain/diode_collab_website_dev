## Diode Collab vs Dropbox: Secure File Sharing and Storage

[Dropbox](https://www.dropbox.com/) is a popular cloud storage and file-sharing service with 2GB free storage (scaling to terabytes on paid plans), supporting device syncing, collaboration, version history, and offline access, using centralized servers for data management.

[Diode Collab](https://collab.diode.io/) is a decentralized, local-first collaboration platform that emphasizes self-custody and end-to-end encryption for secure file sharing and team workflow. It leverages blockchain networks for censorship-resistant storage with unlimited history, offline access, and no central provider control, ideal for privacy-focused users.

## Diode Collab vs Dropbox: Key Differences at a Glance

| **Aspect** | **Diode Collab** | **Dropbox** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Data Custody & Security** <sup>1</sup>| You control your data, fully encrypted, only you have access | Encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256), but stored in the cloud and accessible by the provider/admins |
| **File History** <sup>2</sup>| No set limits, you keep what you want | Limited history and storage based on your plan |
| **Online/Offline Use** <sup>3</sup>| Works offline, syncs when back online | Needs internet for most features; limited offline access |
| **Sensitive Use** <sup>4</sup>| Built for private, sensitive data without third-party access | Strong protections (2FA, access controls), but not fully private from the provider |
| **Infrastructure & Control** <sup>5</sup>| No central owner, harder to shut down or restrict | Centralized service controlled by the company |
| **Overall Takeaway** <sup>6</sup>| Privacy-first, user-controlled | Easy to use, but requires trust in the provider |

Teams gain full ownership of their files and documents with Diode Collab, due to the to end-to-end encryption, full file history, offline functionality, and a blockchain-backed design, ensuring collaboration stays private, reliable, and free from centralized servers.

### **Data Custody & Security**<sup>1</sup>

Users maintain complete control over their data in Diode Collab through self-custody on devices and decentralized networks, secured by full end-to-end encryption where only authorized parties have access.

### **File History**<sup>2</sup>

Diode Collab has no limits on file or version history, allowing teams to retain records indefinitely based on their local or canister storage.

### **Online/Offline Use**<sup>3</sup>

As a local-first platform, Diode Collab enables seamless offline work with peer-to-peer syncing that resumes automatically upon reconnection.

### **Sensitive Use**<sup>4</sup>

Diode Collab excels for confidential data handling, encrypting everything on-device with self-managed keys to eliminate third-party visibility risks.

### **Infrastructure & Control**<sup>5</sup>

Built on a blockchain network, Diode Collab avoids single points of failure, providing censorship resistance without reliance on a central authority.

### **Overall Takeaway**<sup>6</sup>

Diode Collab empowers privacy-centric teams with sovereign, resilient collaboration free from external dependencies.
